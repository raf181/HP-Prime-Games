It's been my habit for many HP calculators to program a simple Blackjack game. I finally got that done for my Prime.

Nothing fancy. No insurance, no splitting, etc. It's about learning to program, not pretending I'm at a real casino!

This works on my Prime with ROM 13865. I believe the ROTATE(grob) and WAIT(-1) options I use are newer, so I can't predict if this program will work on older revisions.

Good luck and have fun!!
